White Rice

Description

This simple recipe has been passed down my family for quite possibly thousands of years. It took a lot of tweaking throughout my family generations to find just the right rice to water ratio, and now I'm passing this recipe on to the world. All you need is a rice, water, and a really expensive rice cooker. Go ahead and try out this tasty rice recipe today!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 1 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp toasted black sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear
  2. Add cleaned rice to rice cooker
  3. Add water
  4. Close lid, and turn on rice cooker
  5. Fluff rice when done
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds

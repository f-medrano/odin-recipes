Milanesa

Description

This milanesa recipe is a staple in many mexican homes, including mine. Paired with a great salsa, this main dish will be sure to satisfy your craving for fried food! Using a minimal amount of ingredients and minimal prep time, it's also a really efficient meal for when you want something tasty but don't have the time. Enjoy the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound of thin steaks
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  • vegetable oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Grind garlic and peppercorns together in mortar
  2. Whisk eggs and stir in garlic/peppercorn mixture
  3. Place breadcrumbs on dish and sprinkle with salt
  4. Dip steaks into egg mixture
  5. Place steaks into breadcrumbs and coat evenly
  6. Heat 1/2 inch of oil on medium-high heat
  7. Fry 3 steaks for 3 minutes on both sides until golden brown.
  8. Place steaks on paper towels to absorb excess oil

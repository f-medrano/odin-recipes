Kimchi

Description

This classic kimchi recipe using nappa cabbage and traditional korean ingredients is essential to your future korean cooking adventures. Created by Maangchi, this recipe makes a superb kimchi that's spicy, salty, sour, and most of all, delicious. Perfect for any korean meal, this side dish will add a flavorful kick to anything you eat with it.I hope you enjoy Maangchi's recipe just as much as I do!

Ingredients

  • 6 lbs nappa cabbage
  • 1/2 cup kosher salt
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp sweet rice flour
  • 2 tbsp turbinado sugar
  • 2 cups radish matchsticks
  • 1 cup carrot matchsticks
  • 7-8 chopped green onions
  • 1 cup chopped buchu
  • 1 cup minari (optional)
  • 1/2 cup minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 minced medium onion
  • 1/2 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped saeujeot (with brine)
  • 2 cups gochugaru
